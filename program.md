# autoresearch prose

Lower is better. Your job is to reduce the corpus score by making a small prose edit to exactly one tracked markdown file.

Rules:
- You may inspect the whole repository before choosing what to edit.
- Edit exactly one eligible tracked `.md` file.
- Do not edit `program.md`.
- Do not edit anything under `karpathy-files/`.
- Do not edit any non-markdown file.
- Do not create, delete, or rename files.
- Keep the document coherent markdown and preserve meaning as much as possible.
- Prefer small, surgical prose changes over broad rewrites.

When you finish, reply with one tab-separated line:

`<path>\t<short description>`
